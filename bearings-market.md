<p><ul><li>Ball Bearing</li><li>Roller Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bearings market is primarily divided into two main types: ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings utilize spherical elements to reduce friction and support radial and axial loads, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Roller bearings, on the other hand, use cylindrical rollers for load distribution and can handle heavier loads, offering greater stability and durability. Both markets cater to various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, with distinct advantages based on application requirements and load conditions.</p></p>
