<p><ul><li>Chute Selector</li><li>Belt Selector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color selectors are essential tools in various industries for sorting and categorizing products based on color attributes. The chute selector operates on the principle of gravity, where items are funneled through a chute, allowing for easy identification and sorting of colored items. In contrast, a belt selector utilizes conveyor belts to transport products past sensing devices that detect color variations, enabling real-time sorting. Both systems enhance efficiency in processes like food production, recycling, and quality control by ensuring accurate color differentiation.</p></p>
