<p><strong>The RF Power Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-based</li><li>Diode Detector-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF power sensors market is primarily divided into heat-based and diode detector-based types. Heat-based sensors measure power levels by converting RF energy into heat, providing accurate power measurements across a wide frequency range, ideal for laboratory and testing environments. In contrast, diode detector-based sensors utilize semiconductor diodes to detect RF signals, offering faster response times and compact designs, making them suitable for real-time monitoring in telecommunications and various RF applications. Both types cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
