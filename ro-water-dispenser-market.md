<p><strong>The RO Water Dispenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU RO Water Dispenser</li><li>POE RO Water Dispenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO water dispenser market is primarily divided into two types: Point-of-Use (POU) and Point-of-Entry (POE) systems. POU RO water dispensers are installed at specific consumption points, providing filtered water directly to faucets or appliances. They are ideal for residential or small commercial use. In contrast, POE RO water dispensers treat water at the entry point of a building, ensuring that all outlets provide purified water. This type is suitable for larger facilities, offering comprehensive filtration for entire households or businesses.</p></p>
