<p><p>The High Technology PCB market includes several specialized types. Multi-layer rigid PCBs feature multiple layers for complex circuitry. Heavy copper PCBs are designed for high-current applications, improving power handling. High-frequency PCBs are optimized for fast signal transmission, essential in RF applications. Other types may include specialty PCBs catering to niche industries or applications.</p></p>
