<p><p>The pyrolysis plant market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Batch Type**: These plants operate on a batch basis, processing a fixed amount of feedstock at one time, ideal for small-scale operations with lower investment costs. </p><p>2. **Semi-Continuous Type**: These systems allow for partial continuous processing, combining batch and continuous features, enhancing efficiency and output without significant operational complexities. </p><p>3. **Fully Continuous Type**: Designed for high-capacity production, these plants process feedstock continuously, maximizing output and efficiency, suitable for large-scale industrial applications.</p></p>
